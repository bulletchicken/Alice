    About

    Who are we?

    We are a team of students based in Ontario, Canada who absolutely love STEM and want to make it easy for people to dive right into it! We are glad to take in other international students into our team and hope to expand Alice Hacks beyond just a hackathon!

    What is Alice Hacks?

    Alice Hacks is an international hackathon that draws inspiration from the classic tale of Alice in Wonderland. The event places a strong emphasis on the power of imagination, which is at the heart of the story. Participants are encouraged to let their creativity run wild and explore new ideas and approaches to problem-solving.

    Why are we different?

    We will primarily focus on helping beginners get their first hackathon started and even have exlusives prizes for them! We will also have a larger weighting on creativity, imagination, as well as goofiness! Do not worry if you are a hackathon veteran either because technicality will still a major factor!

    When is it?

    Alice Hacks is expected to have its first iteration summer of 2023!
